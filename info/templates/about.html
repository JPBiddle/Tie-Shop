About Monty & Turner’s

Welcome to Monty & Turner’s, where tradition meets craftsmanship in the heart of Italian elegance. Our brand is dedicated to bringing you the finest handmade silk ties, each piece a testament to the timeless artistry and meticulous attention to detail that defines Italian heritage.


Our Story

Monty & Turner’s was born out of a passion for elegance and sophistication. Inspired by the classic styles of the past and the luxurious materials of Italy, our founders embarked on a journey to create ties that are not just accessories, but statements of character and refinement. Each tie we offer is a blend of tradition and modernity, designed to elevate your wardrobe and enhance your personal style.


The Craftsmanship

Our ties are handcrafted in Italy by skilled artisans who have honed their craft over generations. We use only the highest quality silk, sourced from renowned mills in Italy, ensuring a product that is both luxurious and durable. Every stitch, fold, and finish is executed with precision, reflecting the mastery of our craftsmen and the timeless elegance of Italian design.


Our Commitment

At Monty & Turner’s, we are committed to excellence. We believe that true luxury lies in the details, and we strive to provide you with ties that are unparalleled in quality and style. Our collection is carefully curated to offer a variety of designs, from classic solids to intricate patterns, each one embodying the spirit of Italian sophistication.


Why Choose Monty & Turner’s?
  • Heritage & Tradition: Rooted in the rich history of Italian craftsmanship.
  • Quality Materials: Premium Italian silk for unmatched elegance and longevity.
  • Handmade Excellence: Every tie is individually crafted to perfection.
  • Timeless Design: A blend of classic and contemporary styles to suit every occasion.
